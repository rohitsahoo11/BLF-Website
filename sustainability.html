Our Responsibility

Fashion with a Conscience

At Biharilal Fashions, we believe that high-quality garment manufacturing shouldn't come at the cost of the environment or the people who make it. Discover our comprehensive approach to sustainability.

A Circular Vision

Minimizing Our Footprint

Sustainability is integrated into every phase of our operations—from the initial sourcing of raw materials to the final finishing processes. We actively monitor and reduce our carbon footprint, water consumption, and energy usage across all our facilities.

We work closely with our partners to implement circular fashion principles, ensuring that waste is minimized and materials are utilized efficiently through advanced CAD layout optimizations.

  • ETP (Effluent Treatment Plant) for zero toxic water discharge
  • 40% of factory power sourced from local solar grids
  • Aggressive fabric scrap recycling program
Human-Centric Approach

Ethical Labor Practices

Our greatest asset is our workforce. We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy for forced labor and child labor. We are deeply committed to providing safe, healthy, and equitable working conditions for every individual in our facilities.

As a WRAP Certified and Sedex SMETA audited manufacturer, we guarantee fair living wages, comprehensive health benefits, and continuous skill development programs for our artisans and floor workers.
