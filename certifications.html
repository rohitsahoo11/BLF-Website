Global Standards

Certifications & Compliance

We operate at the highest global standards, ensuring quality, safety, and ethical production in every garment we manufacture. Our certifications are a testament to our continuous commitment to excellence.

Trust Backed by Verification

Working with international brands requires unwavering adherence to various global protocols. We are regularly audited by independent third-party organizations to ensure our facilities meet strict social, environmental, and quality management criteria.

ISO 9001:2015

Certified for comprehensive Quality Management Systems. This ensures our production processes consistently meet customer and regulatory requirements while actively working to improve operational efficiency.

WRAP Certified

Worldwide Responsible Accredited Production. We maintain a gold-level certificate demonstrating our dedication to safe, lawful, humane, and ethical manufacturing practices across our facilities.

Oeko-Tex Standard 100

Garments and textiles tested for harmful substances. When producing with Oeko-Tex certified fabrics and dyes, we guarantee that the final apparel is safe for human ecology.

GOTS Compliance

Global Organic Textile Standard. For organic apparel lines, we ensure compliance through the entire supply chain, documenting that textiles are made from organic fibers and adhere to strict ecological criteria.

Sedex SMETA

Sedex Members Ethical Trade Audit. We are frequently audited under SMETA pillars encompassing labor standards, health & safety, environment, and business ethics, providing transparency to our partners.

Better Cotton Initiative

We are registered supply chain partners with the Better Cotton Initiative (BCI), supporting the improvement of cotton farming globally through sustainable and more ethical agronomic practices.
